Services & Rates

Everything you need,
all in one place

All rates are based on one pet. Visits include a post-visit update with photos. Reach out for a free custom quote.

Daily Care

Dog Walking

A 30-minute leashed walk tailored to your dog's pace and energy level. Includes a post-visit summary with photos so you know how the walk went. Great for dogs who need daily exercise and mental stimulation while you're at work or away.

$22 per visit

Drop-In Visit

A ~30-minute check-in covering feeding, fresh water, litter or waste cleanup, and quality time with your pet. Includes a post-visit photo update. Perfect for cats, small animals, or dogs who are comfortable alone but need midday attention.

$22 per visit

Extended Drop-In

A 2-hour visit for pets who need more than a quick check-in. Includes a walk, extended playtime, feeding, and light home maintenance such as bringing in mail or adjusting lights. Ideal for high-energy dogs or multi-pet households that need extra attention.

$50 per visit

Overnight Care

Overnight House Sitting (No Pets)

I stay at your home overnight to keep it secure, lived-in, and well-maintained while you're away. Includes mail collection, light upkeep, and regular check-ins. A great option for homeowners who want peace of mind without having pets on the premises.

$50 per night

Overnight Pet Sitting

I stay at your home from evening through morning, providing your pets with a full overnight of care, companionship, and their normal routine. Includes feeding, walks, playtime, and a morning update. The closest thing to having you there — your pets stay comfortable in their own space without the stress of boarding.

$80 per night

About Me

When you're away, your home and pets are in caring, capable hands.

I'm Micah McNutt, a lifelong animal lover based in the Greater Wilmington area with a genuine passion for caring for the pets and homes of the people around me. I started McNutt Pet & House Sitting because I believe every pet deserves attentive, personalized care from someone they can trust, not a crowded boarding facility or a rushed stranger.

Whether it's a midday walk to burn off some energy, a drop-in visit to keep your cat company, an extended afternoon of playtime and home upkeep, or a full overnight stay so your pets never have to leave the comfort of their own home, I'm here for all of it. I offer dog walking, drop-in visits, extended drop-ins, overnight pet sitting, and overnight house sitting across Delaware, Pennsylvania, and Maryland.

Every pet I care for is treated like my own. I keep things consistent with their routines, communicate regularly with photo and text updates, and make sure you feel informed and at ease from the moment you book until the moment you're back home.
